Applied Mathematics · Scientific Computing

Advancing surface PDEs and computational modeling

I develop computational methods for surface PDEs and phase-field models, with applications to biophysical systems including lipid vesicles and fluid-structure interactions. My research combines reduced order modeling, numerical analysis, and scientific computing.

Download CV Browse Publications

Reduced Order Modeling

Efficient computational models for high-dimensional systems with rigorous stability and accuracy guarantees.

Surface PDEs & FEM

Advanced formulations for surface Navier–Stokes–Cahn–Hilliard systems using unfitted and trace finite elements.

Biophysical Modeling

Continuum models for lipid vesicles, domain coarsening, and membrane dynamics validated with experiments.
